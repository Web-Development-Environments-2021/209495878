About me

The site is dedicated to Leticia Cohen Levy.
She was born in 1940 in Morocco and in the 70's moved to Paris where she lives there.
During her life she worked as an accountant in the private business she and her husband ran.
She loves to knit, read thrillers and biographies, bake cakes and cookies and loves her childrens, grandchildren and great-grandchildren.
Her favorite flower is mimosa and it hardens her house.
Her nickname is Mamie Lisette (Grandma in French)
